About Us

Hello my dear friends, my name is Kim from Uganda. I grew up as an orphan boy in the slums of Mityana district.The suffering and hunger I went through, created a sense of joy and happiness into me. This is because it opened my heart towards saving the helpless and orphaned children in my society.So when I reached 16 years, I begun this foundation supporting vulnerable children but I can't handle this alone. So am here to open my society to you so as you can help me support the orphanage home by donating anything you can to these children because our orphanage home really lacks support.

Our Goal

This is part of our journey...
         We want to set up good standards of living for these children by constructing a better orphanage home for them.
         We want to offer the five basic needs for these children i.e shelter, food, medication and education.
      We want to sensitize the natives about human rights.
          We want to create happiness and problem free environment.
